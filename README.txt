User's manual

The frog Jim wants to pass the road and river through!

However, there are fast driving car and deep sea water may cause you life!
You have three chance to get to the other side, can earn as much as score you can,

press the arrow key up, down, left, right to move your frog.
up: move you frog upward.
down: move you frog downward.
left: move you frog left.
right: move you frog right.

beware of the car, if you got hit by a car your life will drop down by one, if you life goes zero, the games is over.
If you successfully pass and get to the other side, you will earn one score! earn as much as score you can before all you life is consumed!
